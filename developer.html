About Me

Hello! My name is Raju, and I am the creator of this Japanese Learner's Hub. As a fellow student of the Japanese language, I know how challenging it can be to memorize vocabulary. I built this tool to help myself and other learners from the Bangla-speaking community to practice and master the N5 words in an interactive and efficient way.

My goal was to create a clean, modern, and useful application that you can personalize with your own word lists and study at your own pace. I hope you find it helpful on your journey to learning Japanese!
